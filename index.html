ℹ️ About BHARATIYA BOT

BHARATIYA BOT is an all-in-one Discord bot inspired by top bots like Dyno, UnbelievaBoat, and ApplyBot. It’s built to support Indian Discord communities 🇮🇳 with unique features, but works worldwide!
